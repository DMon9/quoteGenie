EstimateGenie is solo‑built

Hi, I'm Douglas McGill. I designed, coded, deployed, and maintain EstimateGenie end‑to‑end. My goal is simple: remove the friction from creating fast, accurate estimates with a clean workflow and practical AI where it truly helps.

The Story

I built EstimateGenie after spending too much time wrestling with quotes, spreadsheets, and back‑and‑forth emails. Speed and accuracy win work, so I set out to make a tool that turns messy inputs (notes, images, video) into clear, professional estimates—fast.

There’s no big team behind this product—just me. I handle the backend (FastAPI), frontend (static pages + Tailwind), LLM/ vision integration, infrastructure (Cloudflare + Fly.io), and continuous iteration with real‑world feedback.

If you’ve got ideas or run into rough edges, I’d love to hear from you. Your feedback directly shapes what I build next.
