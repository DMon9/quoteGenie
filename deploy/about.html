Revolutionizing Estimation with AI Innovation

Founded in 2023, EstimateGenie emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to eliminate the time-consuming, error-prone process of manual project estimation and replace it with intelligent, accurate, and lightning-fast AI-powered solutions.

Our Story

It all started when our founder, a seasoned contractor, spent countless hours creating quotes for potential clients, only to lose projects due to delayed responses or inaccurate estimates. The frustration was real - talented professionals were losing business not because of their skills, but because of outdated estimation processes.

That's when we assembled a team of AI engineers, industry experts, and UX designers to create something revolutionary. After two years of intensive research and development, EstimateGenie was born - the world's first AI-powered estimation platform that understands context, analyzes images, and generates professional quotes in seconds.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of businesses across 50+ industries, helping them save time, win more projects, and grow their revenue.
