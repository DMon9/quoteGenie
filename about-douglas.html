The Vision Behind EstimateGenie

As a solo developer and entrepreneur, I, Douglas McGill, recognized a critical gap in the industry: the time-consuming, error-prone process of manual project estimation that was holding back professionals across construction, repair, and service industries.

Having witnessed countless skilled professionals lose business opportunities not due to lack of expertise, but because of outdated estimation workflows, I set out to build something revolutionary. EstimateGenie represents months of intensive development, combining cutting-edge AI technology with practical industry knowledge.

From the initial concept to the production deployment you see today, every line of code, every design decision, and every feature has been crafted with one goal: empowering professionals to generate accurate, professional estimates in seconds rather than hours.

My Mission

"To democratize professional estimation through AI, giving every contractor, service professional, and business owner the tools they need to compete and thrive in today's fast-paced market."
