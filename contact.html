Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about EstimateGenie

How accurate are EstimateGenie's estimates?

Our AI delivers 90%+ accuracy by analyzing project images, descriptions, and local market data. The more details you provide, the more accurate your estimate becomes.

Can I integrate EstimateGenie with my existing tools?

Yes! We offer robust APIs and integrations with popular CRM, project management, and accounting tools. Our technical team can help with custom integrations.

What types of projects does EstimateGenie support?

We support 50+ industries including construction, renovation, landscaping, plumbing, electrical work, and more. Our AI is trained on millions of project data points.

Is there a free trial available?

Absolutely! Start with 5 free quotes, no credit card required. Professional and Business plans also include 7-day free trials.

How secure is my project data?

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 compliant and follow industry best practices. You retain full ownership of your data.
