1. Land on page
2. See animated background
3. Read headline

5. Scroll to upload section
6. Maybe try demo
7. See pricing, features, testimonials
8. Decide to sign up

**Steps to demo**: 4-6 actions

### Minimalist Design User Journey
1. Land on page
2. See quote builder immediately
3. Upload image or try demo

5. Sign up

**Steps to demo**: 2-3 actions

**Winner**: âœ… Minimalist (50% fewer steps)

## Conversion Optimization

### Original Design
- **Multiple CTAs**: Try Free, Watch Demo, Get Started, etc.
- **Scattered focus**: Features, pricing, testimonials compete
- **Long page**: Users must scroll extensively
- **Decision fatigue**: Too many options

### Minimalist Design
- **Single focus**: Quote builder
- **Clear hierarchy**: Primary CTA obvious
- **Immediate value**: Demo works instantly
- **Simplified choice**: Try it or sign up

**Winner**: âœ… Minimalist

## When to Use Each Design

### Use Original Design When:
- You need to showcase many features
- You have extensive social proof to display
- Your audience expects comprehensive information
- You're targeting enterprise customers
- SEO content is priority

### Use Minimalist Design When:
- You want fast conversion
- Your product has a clear demo
- Mobile traffic is significant
- You want clean, modern aesthetic
- Speed and simplicity are priorities
